Ayurveda is the time tested traditional system of medicine of India. The term 'Ayurveda' meaning 'the knowledge of life' comprises of two Sanskrit words viz 'Ayu' meaning 'Life' and 'Veda' meaning 'Knowledge' or 'Science'.

Yoga combines physical, breathing, and meditative practices for well-being, while naturopathy uses natural remedies and lifestyle changes to enhance the body's self-healing.

Unani medicine is a traditional system of healing based on the balance of bodily humors and natural remedies. It emphasizes holistic treatment through diet, herbs, and lifestyle adjustments.

Siddha is an ancient South Indian medical system emphasizing holistic health through the balance of elemental forces, using herbs, minerals, and lifestyle practices. It focuses on both physical and spiritual well-being.

Sowa Rigpa is a traditional Tibetan system of medicine that integrates herbal remedies, dietary guidelines, and lifestyle practices to balance the body's energies and promote health. It emphasizes a holistic approach to healing and well-being.
